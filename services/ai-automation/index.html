Do you use Zapier or custom code?

We use the most efficient tool for the job. Often it's a hybrid: Zapier or Make.com for orchestration, and custom Python/Node.js scripts for complex LLM logic and RAG pipelines.

How do you handle data privacy?

We deploy agentic workflows using your own private API keys and secure databases. We maintain a zero-training policy—your proprietary business data is never used to train public models.

Will this replace my employees?

No. AI is a leverage tool. Our goal is to automate the low-leverage, repetitive tasks (like data entry or lead sorting) so your team can focus on high-impact strategy and creative direction.

How do you measure Success?

By hours saved. We audit your current manual processes and set KPIs based on the reduction of operational man-hours and the increase in lead processing speed.
