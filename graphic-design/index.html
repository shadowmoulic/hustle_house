Common Queries

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about working with our design team.

What file formats will I receive?

We provide all industry-standard formats including AI, EPS, SVG for vectors, and high-res PNG/JPG for web use.

How many revisions do I get?

Our standard packages include 2-3 rounds of revisions to ensure the final design perfectly aligns with your vision.

What is the typical turnaround time?

Simple designs like posters take 2-3 days, while complex brand identities can take 1-2 weeks of deep work.

Do you handle print production?

We provide print-ready files (CMYK with bleed), but we don't handle the physical printing ourselves.
